About · 关于
Dr. Yunlin Yang (Caspar) is Executive Director and Lecturer at Stamford International University, Hua Hin Campus. Holding a PhD in Finance from the University of Newcastle Upon Tyne, he specialises in making complex financial and quantitative concepts accessible through structured, student-centred pedagogy.

This site hosts bilingual interactive teaching tools built for undergraduate students — designed to complement lectures with discovery-based learning.

杨云麟博士（Caspar）现任斯坦福国际大学华欣校区执行总监兼讲师，专注于通过结构化、以学生为中心的教学法，使复杂的金融与定量概念变得易于理解。
