什么是金融？
What is Finance?
Live Group Activity · 实时小组探究活动  |  25–30 min · 3–5 students per team
About this activity · 活动说明
🇬🇧 English Your instructor has assigned you to a team. This activity has no single correct answer. By exploring three everyday financial products together, you will discover a definition of finance yourselves.

Your teammates' answers sync to your screen in real time — work together!
🇨🇳 中文 老师已为你分配小组。本活动没有唯一标准答案。通过一起探究3种日常金融产品，你们将自己归纳出金融的定义。

队友的答案会实时同步到你的屏幕——共同协作！
How it works · 活动流程
①
Pick 3 financial products from daily life — as different as possible.
从日常生活中选3种差异较大的金融产品。
②
Analyse each product — who uses it, what moves, what each party gains.
分析每种产品——谁在使用、流动的是什么、各方获得什么。
③
Find what all three share — the most important thinking step.
找出3种产品的共同之处——最关键的思考步骤。
④
Write and vote for your team definition of finance.
各自写出定义，然后投票选出小组最佳定义。
💡 Tip: Don't define "finance" right away — let it emerge from your discussion · 不要急于定义，让答案从讨论中浮现

Core Question · 核心问题
What do all financial products ultimately do? 所有金融产品最终在做什么？
💡 Think: who has resources now vs who needs them — across time or space. · 谁现在有资源 vs 谁需要资源——跨时间或跨空间。

👥 All Team Definitions · 全组定义 (Vote for the best one · 投票选出最佳)
  • Resources (capital/money) — what is being moved · 资源（资本/钱）
  • Allocation — from those who have to those who need · 配置——从拥有者到需要者
  • Time and/or space — now vs. future, or across parties · 时间/空间——跨越现在与未来，或不同个人/地区

❌ Incorrect · 密码错误
Finance is the mechanism for allocating economic resources (capital/money) across time and space.
  • Across time: present ↔ future (saving, borrowing, investing)
  • Across space: between individuals, firms, industries, or regions
金融是配置经济资源（资本/钱）的机器，帮助人们实现跨越时间、跨越空间的资源配置。
  • 跨越时间：资源在现在和未来之间的配置（储蓄、借款、投资）
  • 跨越空间：资源在不同个人、企业、产业、地区之间的配置

Q4
