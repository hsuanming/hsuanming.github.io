Pick 3 financial products from daily life — as different as possible.

Find what all three share — the most important thinking step.

④
Write your definition of finance in one sentence.

Core Question · 核心问题
What do all financial products ultimately do? 所有金融产品最终在做什么？
💡 Think: who has resources now vs who needs them — across time or space. · 思考：谁现在有资源 vs 谁需要资源——跨时间或跨空间。

✍️ Your definition: 你的一句话定义：
0 words · 0字
A strong definition includes:
  • Resources (capital/money) — what is being moved · 资源（资本/钱）
  • Allocation — from those who have to those who need · 配置——从拥有者到需要者
  • Time and/or space — now vs. future, or across parties/regions · 时间/空间——跨越现在与未来，或不同个人/地区

❌ Incorrect · 密码错误
Finance is the mechanism for allocating economic resources (capital/money) across time and space.
  • Across time: present ↔ future (saving, borrowing, investing)
  • Across space: between individuals, firms, industries, or regions
金融是配置经济资源（资本/钱）的机器，帮助人们实现跨越时间、跨越空间的资源配置。
  • 跨越时间：资源在现在和未来之间的配置（储蓄、借款、投资）
  • 跨越空间：资源在不同个人、企业、产业、不同地区之间的配置
