Step 1 · 第一步
Welcome & Orientation
活动说明与分组
🇬🇧 English Form a group of 3 students. This activity has no single correct answer. Your goal is to think together: by exploring three financial products you already know, you will discover a definition of finance yourselves.

The best learning happens through discussion — share your ideas, challenge each other, and build a shared understanding.
🇨🇳 中文 3人组成一组。本活动没有唯一的标准答案。 目标是共同思考：通过分析3种你们已经熟悉的金融产品，自己归纳出金融的定义。

最好的学习来自讨论——分享想法、互相质疑、共同建立理解。
How This Works · 活动流程
①
Pick 3 financial products from daily life — choose products that are as different from each other as possible.
从日常生活中选择3种金融产品——尽量选择差异较大的产品。
②
Explain how each product is used — who uses it, what moves between parties, and what each side gains.
说明每种产品的使用方式——谁在使用、双方之间流动的是什么、各方获得什么。
③
Find what all three have in common — this is the most important step. Look for shared patterns.
找出3种产品的共同之处——这是最关键的步骤，寻找共同规律。
④
Use those commonalities to define finance in one sentence. Compare your definition with the class.
从共同点出发，用一句话总结金融的定义，并与全班比较。
💡
Tip for your group: Resist the urge to define "finance" right away. Work through the products first — the definition will emerge naturally from your comparison. 小组提示：不要急于直接定义"金融"。先分析产品，定义会从比较中自然浮现。
Step 2 · 第二步
Choose Your 3 Financial Products
选择你们的3种金融产品
🇬🇧 English From the options below, pick exactly 3 different financial products your group has used or knows about. Try to choose products that are as different as possible — the more variety, the richer your comparison will be.
🇨🇳 中文 从以下选项中选择3种不同的你们使用过或了解的金融产品。 尽量选择差异较大的产品——产品差异越大，比较时的发现越丰富。
请选择3种产品 · Please select 3 products
✏️
Own examples welcome! If your group knows a financial product not listed here, you may use it — real personal experience makes the best examples. 欢迎使用自己的例子！如果你们熟悉上表以外的金融产品，完全可以使用——来自个人经历的例子往往最有说服力。
Step 3 · 第三步
How Is Each Product Used?
每种产品是如何使用的？
🇬🇧 English For each of your 3 selected products, discuss and type your answers to the three questions below. Spend about 2 minutes per product. Brief answers are fine — the goal is to record your group's thinking.
🇨🇳 中文 针对每种选定的产品，讨论后将答案填写在下方文本框中。 每种产品大约2分钟。简短回答即可——重点是记录小组的思考过程。
建议时间 · Suggested time：6–8 minutes total · 共6–8分钟（约2分钟/每种产品 · approx. 2 min per product）
🔍
Key observation: After filling in all three products, notice — do your answers follow a similar pattern across all products? 关键观察：填完三种产品后，注意——三个问题的答案在所有产品中是否呈现出相似的规律？
Step 4 · 第四步
Find the Common Thread
寻找共同之处
🇬🇧 English Compare your three products. Check every trait below that applies to all three of your chosen products. A trait only counts if it is true for every single one — not just one or two. This is the most important thinking step.
🇨🇳 中文 对比你们的三种产品。勾选以下所有三种产品都具备的特征。 只有当某个特征在你们每一种产品中都成立时才勾选。 这是本活动最重要的思考环节。
Core Question for Your Group · 小组核心问题
Look at the traits you checked. What do they all have in common? Try to describe in one or two sentences: what do all financial products ultimately do? 观察你们勾选的特征，这些特征共同说明了什么？试用一两句话描述：所有金融产品最终在做什么？
💡 Hint · 提示： Think about who has resources now vs who needs them, and how the product helps connect them across time or space.
思考"谁现在有资源"vs"谁需要资源"，金融产品如何在时间或空间上将两者连接起来。
Step 5 · 第五步
Your Group's Definition
写出你们小组的金融定义
🇬🇧 English Based on the common traits you identified, write one sentence that defines what "finance" is. There is no single correct answer. A good definition captures what financial products fundamentally do.

Write your definition first, then use the password to check the reference.
🇨🇳 中文 根据你们找到的共同特征，用一句话写出"金融"的定义。 没有唯一正确答案。好的定义应该反映金融产品的本质功能。

先写出你们的定义，再用密码查看参考答案。
✍️ Your group's definition of finance: 你们小组对"金融"的定义：
A strong definition of finance usually includes three elements:
  • Resources (capital / money) — what is being moved between parties / 资源（资本/钱）——在参与方之间流动的是什么
  • Allocation — the process of moving resources from where they exist to where they are needed / 配置——将资源从拥有者转移到需要者的过程
  • Time and / or space — this movement occurs across time (now vs. future) or across space (between different individuals, firms, regions) / 时间/空间——这种流动跨越时间（现在与未来）或跨越空间（不同个人、企业、地区之间）

Finance is the mechanism for allocating economic resources (capital / money) across time and space.
  • Across time: allocating resources between the present and the future — e.g., saving, borrowing, investing
  • Across space: allocating resources between different individuals, firms, industries, or regions
金融是配置经济资源（资本/钱）的机器，帮助人们实现跨越时间、跨越空间的资源配置。
  • 跨越时间的资源配置：资源在现在和未来之间的配置（储蓄、借款、投资）
  • 跨越空间的资源配置：资源在不同的个人、企业、产业、不同地区之间的配置

Ask these three questions about your group's definition:
  • Does it mention resources / capital / money? · 有没有提到资源/资本/钱？
  • Does it mention movement or allocation of those resources? · 有没有提到资源的流动或配置？
  • Does it mention time (present vs. future) or space (different parties or places)? · 有没有提到时间（现在与未来）或空间（不同主体或地区）？
如果三项都包含，你们的定义已经非常完整了！
如果缺少某一项，思考一下：你们选的产品中有没有体现那个维度？

If all three are present — your definition is excellent!
If one is missing — look back at your products and ask: does any of them show that dimension?
Step 6 · 第六步
Class Debrief & Reflection
全班讨论与反思
📋 Instructor Guide · 教师引导提示 Invite 2–3 groups to share their one-sentence definition. Write key phrases on the board. Guide students to notice whether each definition mentions: resources, movement / allocation, and time / space. If not, prompt with: "Does a bank loan move money through time or through space — or both?" 邀请2–3组分享他们的一句话定义，将关键词板书。引导学生观察：每个定义是否包含"资源""流动/配置""时间/空间"三要素。若有缺失，追问："银行贷款是跨越时间、跨越空间，还是两者都有？"
Debrief Questions · 讨论问题
Q1
Which of your three products was the hardest to explain? What did that difficulty reveal about how the product actually works? 你们的3种产品中，哪种最难解释？这种困难揭示了什么？
Q2
Did any two groups choose the same product but describe it differently? Why might the same product be understood in different ways? 有没有两组选了相同的产品，但描述方式不同？为什么同一种产品会有不同的理解？
Q3
Can you think of a product that looks like a financial product but does not actually reallocate resources across time or space? 你能想到一种看起来像金融产品，但实际上不涉及跨时间或跨空间资源配置的例子吗？
Q4
Based on today's discussion: why do you think finance exists as a field? What problems would occur if financial products did not exist? 根据今天的讨论：你认为金融为什么存在？如果没有金融产品，社会会出现什么问题？
