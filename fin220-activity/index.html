Step 1 · 第一步
Welcome & Orientation
活动说明与分组
🇬🇧 English Form a group of 3–5 students as assigned by your instructor. This activity has no single correct answer. Your goal is to think together: by exploring three financial products you already know, you will discover a definition of finance yourselves.

The best learning happens through discussion — share your ideas, challenge each other, and build a shared understanding.
🇨🇳 中文 按老师安排，3至5人组成一组。本活动没有唯一的标准答案。目标是共同思考：通过分析3种你们已经熟悉的金融产品，自己归纳出金融的定义。

最好的学习来自讨论——分享想法、互相质疑、共同建立理解。
How This Works · 活动流程
①
Pick 3 financial products from daily life — choose products that are as different from each other as possible.
从日常生活中选择3种金融产品——尽量选择差异较大的产品。
②
Explain how each product is used — who uses it, what moves between parties, and what each side gains.
说明每种产品的使用方式——谁在使用、双方之间流动的是什么、各方获得什么。
③
Find what all three have in common — this is the most important step. Look for shared patterns.
找出3种产品的共同之处——这是最关键的步骤，寻找共同规律。
④
Use those commonalities to define finance in one sentence. Compare with the class.
从共同点出发，用一句话总结金融的定义，并与全班比较。
💡
Tip: Resist the urge to define "finance" right away. Work through the products first — the definition will emerge naturally. 提示：不要急于直接定义"金融"。先分析产品，定义会从比较中自然浮现。
Step 2 · 第二步
Choose Your 3 Financial Products
选择你们的3种金融产品
🇬🇧 English Pick exactly 3 different financial products your group has used or knows about. Try to choose products that are as different as possible — the more variety, the richer your comparison.
🇨🇳 中文 选择3种不同的你们使用过或了解的金融产品。尽量选择差异较大的产品——差异越大，比较时发现越丰富。
请选择3种产品 · Please select 3 products
✏️
Own examples welcome! If your group knows a product not listed, you may use it. 欢迎使用自己的例子！如果你们熟悉上表以外的产品，完全可以使用。
Step 3 · 第三步
How Is Each Product Used?
每种产品是如何使用的？
🇬🇧 English For each product, discuss and type your answers to the three questions. Spend about 2 minutes per product. Brief answers are fine.
🇨🇳 中文 针对每种产品，讨论后将答案填写在文本框中。每种产品约2分钟。简短回答即可。
建议时间 · Suggested time：6–8 minutes total · 共6–8分钟
🔍
Key observation: After filling in all three products — do your answers follow a similar pattern across all of them? 关键观察：填完三种产品后，三个问题的答案是否呈现出相似的规律？
Step 4 · 第四步
Find the Common Thread
寻找共同之处
🇬🇧 English Check every trait below that applies to all three of your products. A trait only counts if it is true for every single one.
🇨🇳 中文 勾选以下所有三种产品都具备的特征。只有当某特征在每一种产品中都成立时才勾选。
Core Question · 小组核心问题
Look at the traits you checked. Try to describe in one or two sentences: what do all financial products ultimately do? 观察你们勾选的特征，试用一两句话描述：所有金融产品最终在做什么？
💡 Hint · 提示： Think about who has resources now vs who needs them, and how the product connects them across time or space.
思考"谁现在有资源"vs"谁需要资源"，金融产品如何在时间或空间上连接两者。
Step 5 · 第五步
Your Group's Definition
写出你们小组的金融定义
🇬🇧 English Based on the common traits you identified, write one sentence defining what "finance" is. Write your definition before opening any reference below.
🇨🇳 中文 根据你们找到的共同特征，用一句话写出"金融"的定义。请先写出定义，再展开下方参考卡片。
✍️ Your group's definition of finance: 你们小组对"金融"的定义：
0 words · 0字
A strong definition of finance usually includes three elements:
  • Resources (capital / money) — what is being moved · 资源（资本/钱）——被流动的是什么
  • Allocation — moving resources from where they exist to where they are needed · 配置——从拥有者转移到需要者
  • Time and / or space — across now vs. future, or across different individuals/firms/regions · 时间/空间——跨越现在与未来，或不同个人/企业/地区

Finance is the mechanism for allocating economic resources (capital / money) across time and space.
  • Across time: allocating resources between the present and the future — saving, borrowing, investing
  • Across space: allocating resources between different individuals, firms, industries, or regions
金融是配置经济资源（资本/钱）的机器，帮助人们实现跨越时间、跨越空间的资源配置。
  • 跨越时间：资源在现在和未来之间的配置（储蓄、借款、投资）
  • 跨越空间：资源在不同个人、企业、产业、不同地区之间的配置

Ask these three questions about your definition:
  • Does it mention resources / capital / money? · 有没有提到资源/资本/钱？
  • Does it mention movement or allocation? · 有没有提到流动或配置？
  • Does it mention time (present vs. future) or space (different parties or places)? · 有没有提到时间或空间？
If all three are present — excellent! If one is missing, look back at your products and ask: does any of them show that dimension?
如果三项都有——非常好！如果缺少某项，回看你们的产品：有没有体现那个维度？
Step 6 · 第六步
Class Debrief & Reflection
全班讨论与反思
📋 Instructor Guide · 教师引导提示 Invite 2–3 groups to share their definition. Write key phrases on the board. Guide students to notice whether each definition mentions: resources, movement / allocation, and time / space. If not, prompt: "Does a bank loan move money through time or through space — or both?" 邀请2–3组分享定义，关键词板书。引导学生观察是否包含"资源""流动/配置""时间/空间"三要素。若缺失，追问："银行贷款是跨越时间、跨越空间，还是两者都有？"
Debrief Questions · 讨论问题
Q1
Which product was hardest to explain? What did that difficulty reveal? 哪种产品最难解释？这种困难揭示了什么？
Q2
Did any two groups describe the same product differently? Why? 有没有两组对同一产品描述不同？为什么？
Q3
Can you think of something that looks financial but does not reallocate resources across time or space? 你能想到一种看似金融产品，但实际上不涉及跨时间或跨空间资源配置的例子吗？
Q4
Why does finance exist? What problems would occur without financial products? 金融为什么存在？没有金融产品，社会会出现什么问题？
